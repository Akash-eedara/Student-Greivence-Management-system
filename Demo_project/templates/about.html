Student Grivience Management System

The Student Grievances Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to address and resolve issues raised by students within an educational institution. This system provides a streamlined and transparent process for students to submit their grievances, ranging from academic concerns to interpersonal conflicts. Through an easy-to-use interface, students can log complaints, attach relevant documents, and track the status of their grievances in real-time. The system facilitates efficient communication between students, faculty, and administration, ensuring a prompt and fair resolution to enhance the overall student experience and maintain a positive learning environment.
